Bunny Feeder

Category: Academic project

About: I created a game that allows the player to control the movement of the bunny character. The objective is to feed the bunny with carrots until a 100 points are reached. The carrots award 10 points, and socks, which are inedible, deduct a life. The bunny begins a new game with 3 extra lives.

To keep things interesting, every now and then, a rare magical sock will appear that awards an extra life, and a rare poisonous carrot will appear that deducts a life. The game is over when either the bunny is full, or when the bunny is dead. Players have the option of saving the current stats, and reloading them at a later point.

Originally I used hand drawn images of the bunny, carrot, and sock to keep the game feeling personal. I ended up changing this to use pixelated images to give it a retro feel.

I used JSwing in order to implement the UI, and JUnit testing to write an exhaustive test suite for the program.
