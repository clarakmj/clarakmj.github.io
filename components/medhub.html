MedHubt

Category: Hackathon project

About: My hackathon partner and I wanted to create an app that will allow the user to complete all admin-related tasks of a healthcare professional in one convenient location. Having worked as a Registered Nurse, I know the difficulties of working with a compassionate transfer patient who comes in with little to no history in the database. Often, this can complicate and endanger patient care.

I used React.js, HTMl, and CSS in order to design and implement the dashboard, patient details, and admission pages of the app.
