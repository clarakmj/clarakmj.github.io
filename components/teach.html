Nursing Clinical Instructor

Category: Work Experience

About: I find teaching very rewarding, which was why I pursued teaching as a nurse. I taught with the UBC School of Nursing for 2 academic terms. I managed 2 groups of about 6-8 students each at varying locations throughout BC, such as Lion's Gate Hospital, Richmond General Hospital, and BC Women's Hospital.

I worked with each of the students to formulate a learning plan that was suitable for each learner and their needs. I collaborated with the hospital staff to ensure smooth communication and teamwork with the students. I demonstrated then supervised the students with clinical skills, such as IV medication administration, or foley removal. I consistently checked all student documentation to ensure accuracy and conciseness.
