Perinatal Registered Nurse

Category: Work Experience

About: I worked as a maternity nurse at Surrey Memorial Hospital for over 3 years. I began my nursing career working with postpartum patients and newborns. I worked my way up from working with low-risk healthy postpartum patients to high-risk patients managing diabetes, hypertension, infection, etc. I completed my BCIT Perinatal Specialty certificate program which allowed me to work full-scope on the unit as a Labor and Delivery nurse. I began seeing intrapartum and antepartum patients, dealing with pre-eclampsia, substance use, premature labor, etc. I often filled in roles such as preceptoring nursing students or orientating new hires.
